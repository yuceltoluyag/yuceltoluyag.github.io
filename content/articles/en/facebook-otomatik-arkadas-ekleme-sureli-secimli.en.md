After the Facebook Bulk Friend Adding (Group + Timed) video, I received a lot of questions like **"Is there a friend adding code?"** I had prepared this post and draft before, but I forgot it due to my forgetfulness and busy schedule. 😅

With Facebook's latest update, it is very important that you be careful when using such applications and codes. Otherwise, you may face the risk of your account being closed, locked, or your IP address being blacklisted. Also, fake accounts you open can be **instantly closed**. Be careful during use; all responsibility arising from use belongs entirely to the user. This code is shared for informational purposes only.

Let me explain what I mean by exaggeration:

1. As soon as you get the code, do not try to add 5000 people at once! 🚫
2. Add between 10-100 people per day and wait for each add to be approved. You can track this from the sent requests section.
3. Whether you add 10 people or 100 people, do not send 1 request per second as I did in the video. Since my account was not important, I sent 1 request per second, but you should perform the adding process at intervals of at least 3-5 seconds.
