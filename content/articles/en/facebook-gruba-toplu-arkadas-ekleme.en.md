## Hello 👋

After the videos I published about two years ago, I started to receive frequent questions on this subject. During this process, I tried to answer all questions coming through Instagram. However, with Facebook's changing design, the process of adding friends to a group has been further restricted. A daily **limit of adding 25 friends** has been introduced, and some changes have been made in the adding interface. Therefore, the old methods no longer work. 😕

The rules in the [Facebook Bulk Friend Adding Version 1](/en/facebook-toplu-arkadas-ekleme/){: target="\_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} method that I mentioned in my previous post are still valid. **Please do not overuse these tools and put your account at risk of spam!** Because I don't want you to return with complaints like "My account fell into spam", "I can't add anyone", "I got banned". 😊 **Use the tools in moderation and carefully!**

## Updated Facebook Group Friend Adding Method 🚀

As shown in the image above:

1. **The first feature** allows you to invite your friends to your group.
2. **The second feature** helps you add your friends to groups you have joined.

### Tools Required for Use 🛠️

- We download the **[Group Invite All for FB](#facebook)** extension.
- We open the group page where we want to add people.
- We run the extension.
- After the friend list comes up, we paste our group link in the relevant area.
- If we want to add an invitation message, we write our message in the **"Enter an invitation note (optional)"** section.
- If you are having trouble finding friends, you can check my [Facebook Automatic Friend Adder + Timed + Selective](/en/facebook-otomatik-arkadas-ekleme-sureli-secimli/){: target="\_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} post.
