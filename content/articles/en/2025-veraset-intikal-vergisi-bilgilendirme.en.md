## Information About the 2025 Inheritance and Transfer Tax 📈

The **2025 Inheritance and Transfer Tax** is an important regulation that determines how taxes are applied on lottery prizes and similar earnings. The change made as of this year directly concerns many citizens, especially regarding games of chance and prize winnings.

With the new regulation, a significant increase has been made in the tax exemption limit, and the scope of the tax taken from lottery prizes has been redefined. So, who does this change affect and how? Let's take a look at the details together.

---

## Changing Tax Exemption: What Is the New Limit? 💡

As of 2025, the **exemption amount** applied to **lottery prize income** within the scope of the Inheritance and Transfer Tax has been increased.

📌 Old exemption limit: **37,059 TL**
📌 New exemption limit (as of 01.01.2025): **53,339 TL**

This change expands the tax exemption limit, especially for winnings obtained from lotteries, sweepstakes, contests, and other prize types. This means that higher prize amounts will now be exempt from tax.

| Year | Exemption Limit | Tax Rate |
| ---- | --------------- | -------- |
| 2024 | 37,059 TL       | 20%      |
| 2025 | 53,339 TL       | 20%      |

---

### How Are Amounts Exceeding the Exemption Taxed? 🧮

According to the new regulation, a **20% tax deduction** is applied to all prizes **exceeding 53,339 TL**. This tax is calculated directly on gross winnings, and the remaining amount after the deduction is paid to the individual.

#### Example Calculation on Nesine.com:

- **Prize won:** 70,000 TL 💵
- **Exemption limit:** 53,339 TL ✅
- **Taxable portion:** 70,000 - 53,339 = 16,661 TL 🧾
- **Deducted tax (20%):** 3,332.20 TL 📉
- **Net payment:** 66,667.80 TL 💰

As seen in this example, the tax is only taken from the portion exceeding the set limit; thus, small prize winners are not affected by the tax.

---

### Who Is Affected? 🎯

This regulation is particularly applicable in the following situations:

- Winners of prizes from the National Lottery and similar games of chance 🎰
- Individual prize winners from TV shows, contests, or social media sweepstakes 📺
- Individuals receiving cash prizes from associations, foundations, or organizations 🏛️

This means that this tax is now applicable not only to inheritance or donations but also to **unrequited cash earnings**.

---

## Why Is It Important? What Does This Change Mean? 🤔

This change aims both to increase state tax revenues and to protect individuals who win lower prize amounts. The increase in the exemption amount is considered a positive development in terms of **fair tax application**.

At the same time, the more transparent and regular taxation of high prizes will contribute to preventing the unregistered economy.

---

### Key Points to Keep in Mind ⚠️

- In the event of winning a prize, the tax deduction is made before the payment is processed.
- The exemption limit may be updated annually, so it is important to check the current figures every year.
- This tax deduction is the final taxation for the winner and does not create an additional tax payment obligation.

---

## Conclusion: A Transparent and Up-to-Date Tax System Benefits Everyone ✨

The inheritance and transfer tax exemption change that came into effect with 2025 is a positive development, especially for individuals who win prizes and lotteries. **Winnings up to 53,339 TL are exempt from tax**, while the exceeding parts are taxed fairly.

Understanding this regulation and correctly planning prize incomes benefits both individuals and organizations. Taking these updates into account in the new year will help you prevent financial surprises.

📌 Remember: Tax awareness is the first step to protecting your earnings.

## Frequently Asked Questions ❓

### What is the Inheritance and Transfer Tax? 📜

Inheritance and Transfer Tax is a tax levied on earnings acquired through inheritance or unrequited gains.

### How much is the lottery/prize tax in 2025? 💰

Lottery prizes up to 53,339 TL are exempt from tax. A 20% tax rate is applied to the portion exceeding this amount.

### How is the tax deduction applied? ✂️

The tax is automatically deducted from the gross earnings before payment is made and paid to the tax office by the organization handling the winnings.

### Is the exemption limit updated every year? 📅

Yes, the Inheritance and Transfer Tax exemption limit is updated and increased every year based on the revaluation rate.
