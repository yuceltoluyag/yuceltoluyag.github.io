Hello, for those who want to **make money** on the internet, I am going to talk about a system that I also use. Earn.com is a platform where you can earn money without making any investment. It offers users the opportunity to make money by filling out surveys or joining groups.

## How Does Earn.com Work?

Earn.com partners with survey companies to send you surveys. Once you complete these surveys, you earn at least 1 dollar. So, how does this process work?

## Creating a Wallet

The system usually pays via Bitcoin and Ethereum. Therefore, you may need a crypto wallet. You can create your wallet at: [MyEtherWallet](https://www.myetherwallet.com/){: target="\_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}. Never share your wallet address with anyone and keep it in a safe place.

## Where is the Real Earning?

- You can earn high income by joining groups according to your skills. For example, in technical groups like the PHP group, it is possible to earn $100 per question.
- You must go through a verification process for the groups you want to join. For example, on the [Earn.com Groups](https://earn.com/lists/){: target="\_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"} page, you are asked to prove your skills when applying.
- Users pay to ask questions, and you earn money by answering these questions.

## Why Earn.com?

- It does not require you to make any investment.
- It pays your earnings on time.
- It is an idea that can be applied in Turkey, but there are not many local equivalents.
- Weekly average income: **150-350 TL**
