Hello, in the past, some **JavaScript** codes known as Facebook **group growing methods** were shared. You could invite your friends to a group by pasting these codes into the browser console. However, the Facebook team set the daily friend adding limit to **1000** and the bulk message sending limit to **50** to prevent this situation.

### 📌 Important Points

Even if you perform the adding process manually, Facebook can analyze your speed and **detect bot usage**. Therefore, it is important to perform friend adding operations at certain intervals.

### ⏳ Recommended Adding Intervals

During the testing phase, I determined the most appropriate intervals by using **1K, 2K, and 5K** accounts. Here are the recommended adding intervals:

- Those with **500 and fewer** friends: 7–10 seconds
- Those with **1K – 2K** friends: 13–20 seconds
- **2K and above**: 20–60 seconds

### 🚀 Usage Recommendation

Add the [Group Invite All](#facebook) extension to your browser and perform the friend adding process by choosing between the intervals mentioned above. You can perform other operations in different tabs while keeping this process running in the background.
