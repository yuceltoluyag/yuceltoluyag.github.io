📽️ **Documentaries Every Turkish Youth Must Watch Series 1**

I recommend watching the documentaries **without looking** at the comments. Yet, I know you will look anyway. 😅 However, remember that the comments usually contain ideological debates, discussions about **Mehmet Ali Birand**'s impartiality, and people interpreting historical events from different perspectives.

Nevertheless, despite all the comments, I believe these documentaries must be watched. With these documentaries, you will better understand when and how concepts you often hear in recent years, such as **hidden hands, foreign powers, traitors, coup plotters, reactionaries**, emerged. 📽️

!!! tip "Watching Order: Please watch in this order. Each documentary complements the other. There is only one full episode. Short videos of that day have been added to the 32nd Day channel. However, watch the documentary first. Afterward, you can watch other videos from the same playlist. This way, you can reinforce the events better."
